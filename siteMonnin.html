Frequently Asked Questions

Is chiropractic care safe for my whole family?
Absolutely. Dr. Monnin uses age-appropriate techniques for every patient — from gentle fingertip pressure for infants to full adjustments for adults. Safety is always the top priority.
How soon will I feel results?
Many patients feel improvement after their first visit. Chronic conditions typically take a few sessions to show significant progress, but Dr. Monnin will always give you an honest timeline.
Do I need a doctor's referral?
No referral is needed. You can call and book directly at any time.
What should I bring to my first visit?
Your insurance card, a photo ID, and any relevant medical records or imaging (X-rays, MRI) if you have them. Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing.
