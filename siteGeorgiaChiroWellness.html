Frequently Asked Questions

Does chiropractic care hurt?
Most patients feel immediate relief after an adjustment. Some mild soreness for 24 hours is normal — similar to after a workout.
How many visits will I need?
It depends on your condition. You'll receive an honest estimate — never more visits than necessary. Many acute problems improve in just a few sessions.
Do I need a referral from my doctor?
No referral needed. You can call and book directly at any time.
What should I wear?
Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing works best. Athletic wear is ideal. Avoid stiff jeans or restrictive clothing.
Is chiropractic care safe?
Yes — chiropractic is one of the safest forms of healthcare when performed by a licensed professional. A thorough assessment is always performed before any treatment.
