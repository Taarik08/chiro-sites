Frequently Asked Questions

Does chiropractic care hurt?
Most patients feel immediate relief after an adjustment. Some experience mild soreness for 24 hours — similar to after a workout. Dr. Simmons will always communicate what to expect before he begins.
How many visits will I need?
That depends entirely on your condition. Dr. Simmons will give you an honest assessment — never more visits than necessary. Many acute problems resolve in just a few sessions.
Is chiropractic care safe for my baby?
Yes. Infant adjustments use extremely gentle, fingertip pressure — nothing like adult adjustments. Dr. Simmons is experienced with newborns and has helped many Tifton families with infant-related concerns.
Do I need a referral from my doctor?
No referral is needed. You can call and book directly. However, if you have a complex medical condition, it's always good to keep your primary care physician informed.
What should I wear to my appointment?
Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing is best. Avoid stiff jeans or restrictive clothing. Athletic wear works great.
